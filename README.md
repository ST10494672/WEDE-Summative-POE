## Project Overview  
Grace & Grounds Coffeehouse is a faith-based café in Cape Town founded by Mark Johnson in 2023. The café offers a welcoming space where people can enjoy quality coffee, participate in Bible studies, prayer nights, and community events, and experience God’s love through hospitality and grace.

This website was developed to extend the café’s mission online, providing a warm, inclusive digital space for connection, engagement, and service. It serves as a central hub for visitors to learn about the café, explore its offerings, join events, volunteer, donate, and get in touch.

The site reflects the café’s values: warmth, community, transparency, and spiritual care.

## Website Goals and Objectives  
### Mission  
To provide a peaceful, inclusive space where people connect and experience God’s love.

### Vision  
To build a thriving, faith-centered community where every cup is brewed with love.

### Goals  
- Increase visitor engagement and awareness of services  
- Share menu, events, devotionals, and community updates  
- Encourage volunteerism and donations  
- Provide clear contact and location information  
- Create a user-friendly, accessible, and responsive website  

### KPIs (Key Performance Indicators)  
- Unique visitors per month  
- Event RSVPs and attendance  
- Email subscriptions for devotionals  
- Volunteer applications  
- Donation conversions  

## Key Features and Functionality  
| Feature | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Responsive Design** | Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices |
| **Navigation Menu** | Fixed header with links to all pages (Home, About Us, Menu, Serve & Give, Contact) |
