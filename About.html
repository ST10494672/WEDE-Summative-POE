About Grace & Grounds

Where faith, community, and coffee come together.

Our Story

Grace & Grounds Coffeehouse was born in 2023 from a simple prayer: “Lord, give us a place where people can feel loved, seen, and known.” Founded by Sarah and Mark Johnson in the heart of Cape Town, this café was never just about coffee — it was about creating a space where grace could be experienced in the everyday.

Inspired by Christ’s radical hospitality, we opened our doors with a mission to offer more than caffeine — we offer connection. From the first cup poured to the quiet moments of prayer at the back table, every detail is designed to reflect God’s love in a busy world.

Our Mission

To provide a peaceful, inclusive space where people connect with one another and experience the love of Christ — one conversation, one cup, and one prayer at a time.

Our Vision

To build a thriving, faith-centered community where every interaction is infused with grace, generosity, and purpose — a true sanctuary in the city.
